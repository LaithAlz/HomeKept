Fair questions.

What don't you do?

Licensed-trade work — gas, electrical panels, plumbing repairs, roofing. We inspect, catch things early, and refer you to a vetted partner who bills you directly. We add zero markup, ever.

Can I cancel or pause?

Both, anytime, from your dashboard — no calls, no retention scripts. Refund terms for annual plans are spelled out plainly in your agreement.

What if I don't need a visit some month?

Every visit has a named seasonal reason, plus your-list time for the small stuff you want done. If a date doesn't work, reschedule from the app.

Are materials extra?

Standard consumables — filters, batteries, humidifier pads, touch-up caulk — are included. Specialty parts are billed at literal cost, receipt in your report.

What's the walk-through?

Ninety minutes, free, no obligation. We catalog your home's systems and you get a written plan within a week — keep it or don't.
